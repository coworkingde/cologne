Organizers How-To

If you want to organize a new event here, please get in touch with us to get editing access to the calendar.

The Coworking Space is a self-service space. Meaning that we provide the infrastructure, you take care of the rest.
To organize events we use the calendar and the mailing list. There can only be one event per day and we have space for about 30 people.

Most important: This is your space. treat it like that. :)
