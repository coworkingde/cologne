We are a non-profit Coworking Space located in Cologne providing a great work space for mobile workers. Besides providing a great space to work we provide our space to many community events.

Our aim is to provide an affordable space work in a productive atmosphere. We are dedicated to to the values of Coworking: Collaboration, Openness, Community, Accessibility, and Sustainability in workspaces.
